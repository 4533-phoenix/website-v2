## Overview

This season required us to pick up yellow foam balls (called fuel).
We had to shoot the fuel into the hub on our alliance.
To score, we also could climb the tower for 10, 20, & 30 points. We went
to Hartsville and North Charleston, and currently at 32.1 EPA. We are going to Disctrict
in Anderson.
